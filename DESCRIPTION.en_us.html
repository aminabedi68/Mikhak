Mikhak is a simple semi-handwriting Arabic-Latin sans typeface in 9 weights(equal width in all weights),3 Arabic Dot styles and with a variable version , published for the first time in 2018 on github.

this typeface has round cap and low contrast in both low/high weight masters, and high saturation thickness in black master. The design of the type is round, smooth, low contrast,legible, friendly and optimized for web-like environments.

To contribute, see github page.
